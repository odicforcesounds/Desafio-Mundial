Some Communities

There are communities that, even when using money to achieve means and ends, may not be associated with businesses and, as such, may represent 'brands' and 'logos' without officially being declared as a business. All of them are included as the target of this global challenge.

  • All religious institutions (from any perspective and interpretation)
    Everyone who represents something beyond themselves.
  • All educational institutions, public and private (teachers and students)
  • All sports teams (clubs and associations)
  • All individuals and groups engaged in recreational activities.
