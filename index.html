Using STK, you can only register by using your Google account, upload videos on Youtube within this account and validate your actions, that focus clear objectives: Animal care, Nature care and Human care. All users can create simple videos of simple actions, and they can create Challenges they cannot do it alone. All users can do from 0 to 255 or more actions, for each objective. 3 objectives 3 RGB channels. Those are the colors of your profile.

An extra feature is added to all users, that give them the opportunity to rate others qualities in different contexts of life. Those are the values that others saw on you in some moment of Time.

Group registration is possible to solve the Challenges that individuals alone, cannot. By solving challenges, groups are announced the main website of this application, with their name, website, number of users, solved challenge videos.

By this conditions, we create a World Wide Challenge for Image Propose only, or in another words, a Logo System competition of self promotion.
